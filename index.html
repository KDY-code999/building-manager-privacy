Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Building Manager ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, stores, and uses your information.

Information Stored

The App stores information that you enter, including:

  • Apartment information
  • Tenant information
  • Rental contracts
  • Payments
  • Reports
  • Attached images and documents

This information is stored locally on your device unless you choose to create a backup.

Google Sign-In

If you choose to use Google Drive Backup, the App uses Google Sign-In solely to access your own Google Drive account with your permission.

Google Drive Backup

Backups are stored only in your personal Google Drive account. The developer cannot access your backup files.

Notifications

The App uses local notifications to remind users about rent and application events. Notifications are generated on your device.

Data Sharing

The App does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Security

Reasonable measures are taken to help protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

Your Choices

You may delete your data at any time by removing it from the App or uninstalling the App.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be published on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the contact information provided on Google Play.
